Our Kitchen

Our Story

Situated at 17A Jemide Ave, GRA, Benin City, Chef P Kitchen brings bold, authentic Nigerian cooking to the table, from smoky firewood jollof and egusi soup to sizzling suya platters and refreshing zobo.

Whether you're in for a full sit-down dinner, a quick bite, a group celebration or a late-night feed, we've got you covered 24 hours a day with dine-in, takeaway and delivery.

Wheelchair-accessible facilities, a kids' menu, high chairs, outdoor seating, free parking and all payment methods make us a restaurant for everyone in Benin City.
