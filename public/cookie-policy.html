1What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device (computer, tablet, or phone) when you visit a website. They allow the website to remember your actions and preferences over time, so you don't have to re-enter information every time you visit or move between pages.

2How We Use Cookies

  • To keep you logged in to your account across pages and browser sessions
  • To remember items in your cart while you browse the menu
  • To understand how visitors use our website so we can improve the experience
  • To detect and prevent fraud and unauthorised access
  • To ensure the website functions correctly and securely

3Types of Cookies We Use

Strictly Necessary

Essential for the website to work. Cannot be disabled without breaking core features. Includes session cookies, security tokens, and cart cookies. Legal basis: Required to provide the service you requested.

Functional

Enable enhanced features you choose, such as the "Remember Me" sign-in option. Includes remember-me tokens that keep you signed in for up to 30 days. Legal basis: Consent.

Analytics

Help us understand how visitors interact with our website (e.g. Google Analytics). Data is collected in anonymised or aggregated form and does not identify you personally. Includes page view tracking, device/browser data, and referral sources. Legal basis: Consent. You may opt out at any time.

Payment

Set by Paystack during checkout to prevent fraud and ensure secure payment processing. These cookies are governed by Paystack's own cookie and privacy policy.

5How to Control or Disable Cookies

Browser Settings

Most web browsers let you control cookies through their settings. You can block, delete, or be notified about cookies:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Cookies and site data

Opt Out of Google Analytics

Install the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-on at tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.

Impact of Disabling Cookies

  • You will not be able to stay logged in to your account
  • Your cart may not persist between pages
  • Some features of the website may not function correctly
